Weijie Dai    戴炜杰

I am a PhD student at Tsinghua SIGS, advised by Prof. Yuhan Dong. I work on optical turbulence, free-space optics, and optical wireless communications.

Previously, I received my M.E. from the same department and my B.S. from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). Before my M.E., I spent a year as a Research Assistant at UESTC working on electron field emission, advised by Prof. Kangcheng Qi and Prof. Xiaoju Wang.

Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research.

Research

My research keywords include:

  • Free-space optical (FSO) communications, light detection and ranging (LiDAR)
  • Wavefront engineering in optics and electromagnetism
  • Optical turbulence and scattering phenomena
